Employee Handbook 2026

Welcome to ABC Technologies Pvt. Ltd. This handbook provides information about company policies, employee benefits, work culture, and code of conduct.

Company Information
ABC Technologies is a software development company specializing in Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing, Data Analytics, and Enterprise Software Solutions.

Working Hours
• Monday to Friday
• 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM
• One-hour lunch break
• Flexible work from home available twice a week.

Leave Policy
Employees receive:
• 20 Annual Leave days
• 10 Sick Leave days
• 12 Public Holidays
Unused annual leave can be carried forward for up to 10 days.

Employee Benefits
The company provides:
• Health Insurance
• Life Insurance
• Performance Bonus
• Provident Fund
• Free Technical Certifications
• Annual Learning Budget of ₹25,000

Training Programs
Employees are encouraged to complete the following courses:
• Python Programming
• Machine Learning Fundamentals
• Deep Learning
• Cloud Computing using AWS
• Cybersecurity Basics

Performance Evaluation
Employees are evaluated every six months based on:
• Technical Skills
• Team Collaboration
• Communication
• Innovation
• Project Delivery

Code of Conduct
Employees must:
• Maintain confidentiality of company information.
• Respect workplace diversity.
• Avoid harassment or discrimination.
• Follow cybersecurity best practices.
• Report security incidents immediately.

IT Security Policy
Passwords must contain at least 12 characters.
Multi-factor authentication is mandatory.
USB storage devices are not allowed without approval.
Sensitive customer data must be encrypted.

Artificial Intelligence Policy
Employees may use AI tools such as ChatGPT, Claude, and GitHub Copilot for development tasks. However:
• Confidential information must never be shared with public AI systems.
• AI-generated code must be reviewed before deployment.
• Employees remain responsible for final decisions.
